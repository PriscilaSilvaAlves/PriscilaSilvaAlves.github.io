Baked Potato

Description: baked potatoes is a easy and fast side dish to you make.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Scallion
  • Parsley

Steps:

First you need to cut each potato in four or six parts. After cut, you can put the potatoes in a baking tray and add the others ingredients as you prefer. The potatos are baked in the oven at medium temperature for 40 minutes.
